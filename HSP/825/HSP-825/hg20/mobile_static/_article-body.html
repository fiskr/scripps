The Basic Wheel

When you’re decorating, choosing a color scheme can be a perplexing task. It’s hard enough whittling down your choice to one or two colors — but then to have to mix and match even more of them into an eye-pleasing scheme? Sometimes considering the whole color wheel is just too much to ask. Before you turn back on the world of color, get a grasp of the color wheel and how it works.

Primary Colors: Red, Yellow and Blue

Secondary Colors: Orange, Green and Violet Secondary colors are created by mixing primary colors.

Three Colors --Triad

A triad scheme is bold but more importantly, it’s balanced. It’s made up of any three colors that form a triangle in the center of the wheel.
