How can multiple eligible users enter using the same computer?

If you have separate user accounts on your computer, simply log in under your user name and enter the sweepstakes.

If there are multiple users logging in as one (no separate user accounts:)

  • Enter using different browsers: If one person enters using Internet Explorer, the next can enter using Chrome, Firefox, Safari or browser of choice.
  • If using the same browser, go into browser settings and delete cookies. Do this before each person completes the entry for the day on each site. Click here to learn more about deleting cookies.
  • Enter using another device, such as a smart phone or tablet.

Issue With Chrome 32

As you may be aware, this is the newest Chrome browser version that was just released, and this functionality is a design issue within the browser itself (not within our sweepstakes page) and therefore will only be resolved with a newer version of Chrome. 

In the interim, you may use your keyboard to type the birth year, month, date, and state abbreviation, or, once you select an entry, you may use the up and down arrow on your keyboard to scroll to the correct selection in the field. For detailed technical information on this issue in the version of Chrome, please see the thread below. 

https://code.google.com/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=279464

Giveaway Start and End Dates

The HGTV Dream Home 2014 Giveaway begins on December 27, 2013 at 9 a.m. ET and ends on February 14, 2014 at 5 p.m. ET. For more details about the home, visit Dream Home Central. 

Eligibility

  • Per the official rules (available December 27) any Scripps employee or product sponsor employee and their respective family members are ineligible to enter the giveaway. Please review the rules for further clarification and details on eligibility.
  • Eligibility Requirements: Military Members of the U.S. Armed Forces are eligible to enter the HGTV Dream Home Giveaway. On the enter page, the "State" field contains AE, AA and AP. Please review the rules for further clarification and details on eligibility.
  •  
  •  
  • Online: Each eligible member of a family may enter online once per day (once on HGTV.com and once on FrontDoor.com), as long as each member has his or her own unique email address. If family members share one email address, only one family member is eligible to enter online.
  • U.S. Mail: In addition to entering the Giveaway online, individuals may also enter via U.S. Mail. There is no limit placed on mail-in entries; however, only one entry per envelope or per postcard will be accepted.
  • Street Address or P.O. Box: Entrants must provide a street mailing address for prize delivery purposes.
  • Address for Mail-In Entries: posted 12/27 along with rules. See rules at that time for specific details.
  • Postcards: Postcards may be mailed as-is or sent in envelopes. Each postcard must meet the USPS minimum size requirement of 5 inches long x 3 ½ inches high.
  • Duplicated Address Labels: Printed address labels are considered mechanical entries and are therefore not accepted. Mass-produced entries are not accepted. The return address can be a stamp or a sticker
    • Duplicate Online Entries How Winner Is Chosen/Notified rules following video Entry Troubleshooting
  • Web Browsers: Compatible desktop browsers are Firefox version 12 or higher, Google Chrome version 19 or 20, Safari version 5, Internet Explorer 8, 9 or 10. For Internet Explorer users, you will also need Adobe Flash version 11.3 or higher.
  • Mobile Device Users – State and Provider Fields: Users may enter using the iOS devices iPad and iPhone, as well as Kindle Fire and the Galaxy tablet.
  • When scrolling on your mobile device, you may need to use two fingers to scroll the state and provider selections on the entry form. Instead of trying to scroll with one finger, try to scroll through the list by pressing two fingers over the appropriate field and swipe in an upward direction with two fingers.
  • Phone Number Format: Your phone number would only be rejected from our sweepstakes entry form if it were entered in a format that was not accepted by the Web form validation. Please make sure that you enter the number as shown in the example provided on the entry form.
  • How do I know my entry has been successfully submitted? You will receive a Thank You page following a successful entry.

  • Taxes: To determine taxes associated with winning the HGTV Dream Home, please seek legal counsel and base calculations on the approximate retail value of the prize, which is provided in the rules, available December 27. 
  • Products: Nearly everything seen in this year’s HGTV Dream Home has associated product resource information available on the Shop HGTV Dream Home 2014 page.
  • Dream Home Central
  • Public Tours of HGTV Dream Home 2014: Visit the Dreams Happen blog to learn more about public tours and order tickets.
  • Architectural Plans: The architectural plans for HGTV Dream Home 2014 are the property of Christopher Rose Architects, PA and are not for sale by HGTV.
